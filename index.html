About Sai Buzurg

Sai Buzurg village is located in Khalilabad Tehsil of Sant Kabir Nagar district in Uttar Pradesh, India. It is situated 20km away from district headquarter Sant Kabir Nagar. Sai Buzurg is the gram panchayat of Sai Buzurg village. The total geographical area of village is 335,787.92 m² (Expected). Sai Buzurg has a total population of 7000 (Expected) peoples.
